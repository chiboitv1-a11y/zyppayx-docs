# Zyppayx – Micro-task Platform Documentation

Zyppayx is an experimental online micro-task and referral platform focused on simple digital activities such as app discovery, website visits, and promotional tasks.

## Purpose
The platform was created to explore how micro-task systems operate in emerging markets and how transparency can improve user trust.

## How the Platform Works
- Users register for free
- Tasks are made available based on advertiser demand
- Users earn rewards for completing valid tasks
- Earnings depend on task availability and verification

## Important Notes
- Zyppayx does not guarantee income
- Task availability may vary
- The platform is still in early development

## Monetization Model
The platform plans to rely on:
- Advertising campaigns
- Affiliate and CPA offers
- Promotional listings

## Transparency
This repository exists to publicly document the platform’s structure, challenges, and improvements.
